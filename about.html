Our Story

Watch-Now

Protecting a Child at Every Step

The Quiet Shelter Empowerment Foundation is a registered not-for-profit organization
that was established in August 2020, in Jimeta Yola, Adamawa State, Nigeria.
Our primary purpose is to provide humanitarian services to communities, aiming to
bring about positive changes and developments.

Mission

To build communities where the hope of the vulnerable and disadvantaged
members is restored through the provision of daily needs like shelter, food,
water, education and empowering them through skill acquisition trainings,
mentorship, for better livelihood

Our Vision

A world of hope, tolerance and social justice where resources are
equitably distributed and all people live in dignity and respect for one another.

Our Goals

A world of hope, tolerance and social justice where resources are equitably
distributed and all people live in dignity and respect for one another.


Our Core Values


We stand by this four core values

Excellence

Mold personalities to be contributors to the society

Transparency

Give power to the needless and the neglected

Respect

Build trustworthy relationship with our Partners and the people we empower

Humanity & Accountability

Grant shelter and clothing to kids around africa
